Why You Should Use UNIX

A System of Endless Possibilities

UNIX is an integral system to our everday living, and I can guarantee that you use it everday without even noticing. UNIX can be described as the backbone of the internet and various other amenitys you probably use like Netflix and chat services. It is also the way that most web hosting is completed due to its capability of being able to have multiple people on a system at once. It also is the foundation for most of bioinformatics, allows the user to not use non graphical user interface tools, and has a much greater chance of reproducibility. Most beginners and veterans of UNIX love it because of its variety of tasks it can preform, its multi-tasking capabilities, ease of use, and its importance to our everday life.

The Pros of Using UNIX

  • You can use Unix for free
  • Secure even for multiple users
  • Can support multiple users on one system
  • It is the prefered method for web hosting
  • Accessing and retriving files is very easy to complete

The Cons of Using UNIX

  • Not user-friendly
  • No official documentation
  • The terminology is very hard to learn when first learning
  • Not all software drivers can be accessed
  • Games are not made with UNIX
