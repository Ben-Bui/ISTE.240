Why Eat at St.John?

St. John Restaurant, located in London, is a celebrated establishment that has made a name for itself in the culinary world. Founded by Fergus Henderson and Trevor Gulliver, it has good a reputation for its distinctive approach to British cuisine. St. John is widely recognized for its "nose-to-tail" dining philosophy. This means that the restaurant makes use of the entire animal, minimizing waste and creating unique and flavorful dishes from a wide range of cuts and ingredients.
Seasonal and Simple: The restaurant's menu is known for its simplicity and reliance on seasonal and locally sourced ingredients. It's an embodiment of the farm-to-table concept, emphasizing freshness and quality.
St. John is famous for its signature dishes, including roast bone marrow with parsley salad, grilled ox heart, and Eccles cake with Lancashire cheese. These dishes exemplify the restaurant's commitment to British culinary traditions and bold flavors.
St. John has received critical acclaim and awards, and it's considered a destination for those seeking an authentic and innovative British dining experience.
