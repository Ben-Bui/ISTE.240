Why Dine at Café Cecilia

Café Cecilia was debut with their higly anticipated chef name Max Rocha, famously known for his recipe seasonal cooking style honed at restaurants like St.John. The restaurant style feel homely with simple wooden tables, open kitchen at the back. This place seem to be the perfect place for a family dinner, the amotsphere seem to be very down to earth, while can also be a well dress fashion place for couple. The staff seem to be relax, control and very friendly toward customer. The Chef really have the love for classic meat dishe with pork and apricot. This place would be a perfect place for breakfast when you want to have a nice meal, or brunch when you are on a date and about to got to the next attraction because the location of this restaurant is in London.
